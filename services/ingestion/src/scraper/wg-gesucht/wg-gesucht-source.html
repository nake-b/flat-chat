Finding a one-room apartment in Berlin – here’s how

One-room apartments are especially popular among singles, students, and commuters. They are often rented out quickly, especially in central districts. Use the list to compare current offers and contact landlords directly.

FAQ: One-room apartments in Berlin

Where can I find affordable one-room apartments in Berlin?

More affordable options are often available in districts such as Marzahn-Hellersdorf, Spandau, or Lichtenberg. In central neighborhoods like Mitte or Charlottenburg, prices are significantly higher.

What is the average rent for a one-room apartment in Berlin?

Rents usually range between €700 and €950 per month, depending on location, size, and amenities.

Which documents should I prepare for a rental application in Berlin?

Typically required are a credit check from SCHUFA or itsmydata, proof of income for the last three months, a certificate confirming freedom from rental debt, and your ID card.

How can I find a one-room apartment in Berlin faster?

Make sure all your documents are ready and set up a free Email Alert to be notified immediately about new listings. Posting your own apartment request also increases your chances, as landlords can contact you directly.
